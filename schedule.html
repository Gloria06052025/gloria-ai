FIN 567 - FinTech Strategy Project

Summer 2025/2026 Course Schedule

Course Structure

This 10-week course is organized into 5 comprehensive modules, each building upon the previous to develop a complete FinTech strategy project. Students will work progressively through research, analysis, strategy development, and implementation planning.

Key Deliverables

Week 2: Project Proposal
Week 4: Research Summary Draft
Week 6: Solution Proposal Draft
Week 8: Strategic Justification Draft
Week 10: Final Strategic Plan & Presentation

Learning Approach

Each module includes peer learning discussions, progressive drafts, and instructor feedback. Students will engage with cutting-edge FinTech concepts while developing practical strategic planning skills.
